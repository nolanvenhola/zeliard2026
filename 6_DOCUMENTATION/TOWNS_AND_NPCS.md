# Zeliard Towns & NPCs Database

Complete guide to all 9 towns, NPCs, shops, and pricing.

---

## Town Overview

### 9 Towns in Order of Appearance

1. **Muralla** - Starting town, King's Palace nearby
2. **Satono** - First underground town (inside caves)
3. **Bosque** - Forest town built in treetops
4. **Helada** - Ice cave town, home of Percel the Shoemaker
5. **Tumba** - Underground graveyard town
6. **Dorado** - Underground merchant town with gold walls
7. **Llama** - Surface town in extreme heat zone
8. **Pureza** - Last underground town (some NPCs under Dark One's spell)
9. **Esco** - **SECRET** underground town near Pureza, best prices!

## NPCs (Non-Player Characters)

### King Felishika
**Location**: King's Palace (Muralla)

**Role**: Grieving king, father of Princess Felisia

**Personality**: Useless, only weeps and grieves

**Interaction**:
- Gives 1000 gold at start
- Otherwise no help
- Always crying over daughter's loss

**Quote**: "I think he ought to get up and do something about it"

---

### The Weapons Master
**Location**: Every town (Weapons Shop)

**Role**: Sells swords and shields, repairs armor

**Personality**: Short-tempered, not friendly

**Interactions**:
- Sells weapons and shields
- Repairs damaged shields (FREE)
- **Loses temper** if you leave without purchase
- Smashes table when angry!
- **Gullible** when you have Crest of Glory
- Refuses to sell Knight's Sword without his family crest

**Special**:
- Trading Crest of Glory = Knight's Sword unlocked
- Only time he's cooperative

---

### The Pope
**Location**: Muralla (1st town) and Esco (last town)

**Role**: Religious leader, healer

**Personality**: Very kind and generous

**Services** (FREE):
- Full health restoration
- Full magical power restoration

**Quote**: "A really kind fellow"

---

### Magic Brewer
**Location**: Every town (Magic Shop)

**Role**: Brews and sells magical potions

**Personality**: Professional, knowledgeable

**Services**:
- Sells consumable magic items
- Provides information about item effects

**Specialty**: Brewing useful potions for cavern combat

---

### The Banker
**Location**: Every town (Bank)

**Role**: Money management, currency exchange

**Personality**: Stubborn, tired of life, always doing accounting

**Expression**: Sour face when talking to you

**Services**:
- Deposit gold (safe storage)
- Withdraw gold (any time)
- Exchange almas for gold (varies by town)

**Moods**:
- **Happy**: When depositing 1000+ gold
- **Annoyed**: Visiting without transaction

**Exchange Controls**:
- Arrows Left/Right: Adjust by 1
- Arrows Up/Down: Adjust by 10

---

### The Sage
**Location**: Every town (Sage's Hut)

**Role**: **Most important NPC** - leveling and save system

**Personality**: Friendly lady, connected to Spirits

**Services**:
- **Level Up**: Increases your power when you have enough XP
- **Grants Spells**: Teaches new magic as you level
- **Increases Health**: Raises max HP based on level
- **Save Game**: "Record your Experiences" (save to .USR file)
- **Load Game**: Can restore saved games
- **Resurrection**: Return here when you die
- **Provides Information**: Useful hints and guidance

**Progress Messages**:
- "You must persevere" (< 50% XP)
- "You must accumulate more experience" (50-74% XP)
- "I see the faint light of the spirits" (75-99% XP)
- Ready to level! (100%+ XP)

**Location**: Usually unmarked building in each town

---

### The Inn Keeper
**Location**: Most towns (Inn)

**Role**: Provides rest and healing

**Personality**: Businessman (charges for free service)

**Services**:
- Overnight stay restores health and magic
- Prices increase by town (30 → 400 gold)

**Quote**: "A true businessman. Who wants to stay in an inn when you can simply rest in the caverns for free?"

**Recommendation**: Waste of money - rest in safe cavern areas instead

---

### Villagers / Townspeople
**Location**: All towns

**Role**: Provide information and gossip

**Personality**: Varies (some helpful, some under Dark One's spell)

**Services**:
- Give advice about caverns
- Provide hints for progression
- Share town history and lore
- **WARNING**: Some are misleading (especially in Pureza and Llama)

**Strategy**: Talk to everyone, visit all buildings

**Interaction**: Press [Spacebar] near person to talk

## Shopping Strategy

1. **Early Game**: Shop in Muralla/Satono (normal prices)
2. **Mid Game**: Use Bosque for good exchange rate
3. **Late Game**:
   - Find Esco for best prices
   - Stock up on everything
   - Use FREE Church healing
4. **NEVER**: Bank in Llama or Tumba (terrible rates)
