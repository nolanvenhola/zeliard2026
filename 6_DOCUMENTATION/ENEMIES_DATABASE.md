## The Caverns (Level 1)

### Frog
- **Almas**: 1 (single coin)
- **Experience**: 5
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Attack**: Quickly hops toward player
- **Damage**: Infinite (contact damage to shield)
- **Strategy**: Easy to kill, just swing before contact
- **Notes**: Won't harm much but damages shield from bumping

### Snail/Slug
- **Almas**: 1 (single coin)
- **Experience**: 2
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Attack**: Slowly crawls toward player
- **Damage**: Infinite (contact)
- **Strategy**: Stronger than frog but moves slowly - plenty of time to kill
- **Notes**: Basic enemy, easy pickings

### Rat
- **Almas**: 1 or 10 (varies)
- **Experience**: 3
- **Difficulty**: Easy / Medium
- **Attack**: Quickly runs toward player, can jump over small obstacles (almas)
- **Damage**: Infinite (contact)
- **Strategy**: Faster than frog, be ready to strike quickly
- **Notes**: More advanced than frog - can navigate obstacles

### Bat
- **Almas**: 1 or 10 (varies)
- **Experience**: 3
- **Difficulty**: Easy / Medium
- **Attack**: Flies from ceiling, attacks with claws, returns to ceiling
- **Damage**: 1 point
- **Strategy**: Must hit while in attack position (only time it's near you)
- **Notes**: Requires timing, attacks in swooping pattern

- **Attack**: Stops at distance and fires toxic spit
- **Damage**: 3 points (spit)

- **Almas**: 1 or 10 (varies)
- **Experience**: 4
- **Difficulty**: Easy / Medium
- **Attack**: Same as normal bat (swooping claw attack)
- **Damage**: 2 points (double normal bat)

- **Almas**: 100 (Almas C)
- **Experience**: 255 (HIGHEST regular enemy XP!)
- **Difficulty**: Easy / Medium

- **Damage**: 5 points
- **Strategy**: Rare encounter, worth lots of XP
- **Notes**: **Endangered species** - only ONE in entire game, never respawns!

### Alligator Man / Woodcutter / Axe Man
- **Almas**: 10 (Almas X)
- **Experience**: 10
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Attack**: Runs backward while throwing axes

## Enemy Behavior Patterns

### Contact Damage Enemies
- Bump into player to damage shield/health
- Examples: Frog, Rat, Fox, Tortoise, Lobster
- Strategy: Kill before contact

### Ranged Enemies
- Fire projectiles from distance
- Examples: Toad, Earthworm, Alligator Man, Zombie, Femme Fatale
- Strategy: Dodge projectiles, close distance, or use shield

### Aerial Enemies
- Attack from above or fly
- Examples: Bat, Spider, Bush Creature, Flying Octopus, Blue Flying Creature
- Strategy: Timing crucial, jump attacks often needed

### Special Mechanics Enemies
- Require specific strategies
- Examples: Green Slime (reproduction), Red Slime (near invulnerability), Flying Jellyfish (head-only damage)
- Strategy: Use correct weapon/spell type

## Alternative Combat Strategy

For enemies requiring head hits (Zombie, Fire Creature, Flying Jellyfish):

**Crouch-Away Technique**:
1. Crouch down
2. Face AWAY from enemy
3. Swing sword
4. Back end of sword swings high enough to hit head

**Pros**: Safer than jumping
**Cons**: Harder to execute, dangerous, requires practice
**Recommendation**: Only for skilled players
